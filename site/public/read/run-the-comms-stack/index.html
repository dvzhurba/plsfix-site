About the models in this demo
The copy scorer is a transparent ~20-feature stand-in for a production pairwise reward model (a 100–300M-parameter encoder trained on millions of A/B comparisons). The item ranker is a real linear ranker — score = Σ weight × feature over long-term interest, short-term intent, CTR, popularity, replenishment and price — and the blender is a real greedy MMR pass with same-cluster demotion. That’s the exact shape production stacks had before gradient boosting and neural rankers, and the lesson survives the upgrade: the features you weight are the policy, and the slate — not the item — is the unit of value. Why a pre-launch reward model instead of just A/B-ing headers? Because post-launch CTR is delayed and noisy on fresh variants with small denominators — it caps how fast the content pool can compound and how fast you respond to wear-out. Roadmap: live LLM generation + LLM judge (tier 2), then a pairwise model trained on players’ choices (tier 3).
