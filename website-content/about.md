# About

I build AI/ML products — and I've done it from a few different seats.

Right now I'm a Principal Product Manager in **country management at an international e-commerce platform**, leading product and the commercial side of a national market.

Before that, at **Avito** — one of the world's largest marketplaces, 60M+ monthly users — I ran communications and buyer-engagement as an AI/ML product. I wrote the board-approved case to turn batch messaging into an ML-driven growth system — LLM-generated content with reward models, ranking and bandit algorithms, uplift modelling, and send-time optimisation across messenger, email, push, and in-app — and served as GenAI lead for marketing and communications. It moved billions of messages a month.

Earlier I founded **BOXR**, a consumer AI coaching app: on-device computer vision and LLM feedback that I built hands-on and took to the top of its category before it was acquired. Before product, I owned strategy and go-to-market for **VK's** Marusya voice assistant and smart-speaker line (a 100M-user portal), and I started out in strategy consulting at **EY-Parthenon**.

Alongside the day job I teach and mentor — an authored Product Management course at Netology, lectures at Imaguru and Product Star, startup mentoring at the Eurasian Business Conference in London, and judging at ITMO University.

MBA from London Business School, MSc in Strategic Management from HEC Paris.
