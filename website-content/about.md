# About

I build AI/ML products — and I've done it from a few different seats.

Right now I'm a Principal Product Manager in **country management at an international e-commerce platform**, leading product and the commercial side of a national market.

Before that, at [**Avito**](/work/ai-communications-platform) — one of the world's largest marketplaces, 60M+ monthly users — I ran communications and buyer-engagement as an AI/ML product. I wrote the board-approved case to turn batch messaging into an ML-driven growth system — LLM-generated content with reward models, ranking and bandit algorithms, uplift modelling, and send-time optimisation across messenger, email, push, and in-app — and served as GenAI lead for marketing and communications. It moved billions of messages a month. I've written up [what's actually achievable, and the architecture that gets you there](/read/ai-ml-crm-2026/).

Earlier I founded [**BOXR**](/work/boxr), a consumer AI coaching app: on-device computer vision and LLM feedback that I built hands-on and took to the top of its category before it was acquired. Before product, I owned strategy and go-to-market for [**VK's**](/work/vk-smart-speakers) Marusya voice assistant and smart-speaker line (a 100M-user portal), and I started out in strategy consulting at **EY-Parthenon**.

Alongside the day job I teach and mentor — an [authored Product Management course at Netology](/recognition/netology-course-author), lectures at [Imaguru](/talks/imaguru-iterating-towards-perfection) and [Product Star](/talks/product-star-pm-in-practice), [startup mentoring](/recognition/getmentor) and an [invited expert seat at the Eurasian Business Conference](/recognition/ebc-london-expert) in London, and [judging at ITMO University](/talks/itmo-judging). I write about AI/ML and product on [HackerNoon](https://hackernoon.com/u/dzhurba).

MBA from London Business School, MSc in Strategic Management from HEC Paris.
