# About

Denis Zhurba builds AI/ML products that reach hundreds of millions of users. He currently leads communications and buyer-engagement products at one of the world's largest online marketplaces (60M+ monthly users), where he authored the board-approved investment case for scaling the communications platform — moving it from batch messaging to an ML-driven growth system using LLM-generated content with reward models, ranking and bandit algorithms, uplift modelling, and personalisation across messenger, email, push, and in-app channels.

Earlier, at VK — one of the largest technology companies in the region — he owned strategy and go-to-market for AI voice assistants and smart-speaker hardware (the Marusya ecosystem), and ran growth on a 100M-user portal. In 2022 he founded BOXR, an AI boxing-coaching app using on-device computer vision and LLM feedback that reached the top of its category before being acquired by a private-equity roll-up. He began his career in strategy consulting at EY-Parthenon.

He holds an MBA from London Business School, an MSc in Strategic Management from HEC Paris, and a BSc in Engineering Management. Alongside his work he teaches and mentors — an authored Product Management course at Netology, lectures at Imaguru and Product Star, startup mentoring at the Eurasian Business Conference in London, and judging at ITMO University.
